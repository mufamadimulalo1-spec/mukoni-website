About Mukoni Integrated Service

Mukoni (Pty) Ltd is a multi-disciplinary South African company providing integrated business support services across cleaning and hygiene management, technical and engineering support, logistics and supply chain operations, and operations coordination services.

The company is committed to delivering professional, reliable, and cost-effective solutions tailored to meet the operational needs of government departments, municipalities, mining operations, industrial facilities, healthcare institutions, educational institutions, and private sector clients.

Mukoni operates through specialized internal divisions, enabling seamless service delivery while maintaining unified management, compliance, and accountability across all projects.

Our approach is built on operational excellence, compliance, quality assurance, client satisfaction, and sustainable long-term partnerships.

Director

Director: Mulalo Mufamadi

The Managing Director of Mukoni (Pty) Ltd is a results-driven business leader with a strong focus on operational excellence, strategic growth, and service delivery across multi-sector business environments.

With experience in business development, operations management, logistics coordination, technical support environments, and client service delivery, the Director leads the company with a vision of building a reliable, scalable, and high-performance enterprise capable of servicing both public and private sector clients.

The Director’s leadership is centered on:

  • Strategic business development
  • Tender acquisition and contract execution
  • Operational efficiency
  • Compliance management
  • Stakeholder engagement
  • Service quality assurance
  • Sustainable business growth

Under this leadership, Mukoni continues to position itself as a trusted contractor capable of delivering integrated solutions across cleaning, engineering support, logistics, and operational coordination.
